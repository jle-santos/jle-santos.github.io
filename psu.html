The DC Power Supply is a variable power supply capable of supplying up to +30V DC at 1A. It was designed, built, and tested in May 2019. The PCB was designed using Altium Designer and components are soldered by hand.

Features

The power supply features are listed below.

- Voltage (Input): 120V AC 60Hz
- Voltage (Output): Up to 30V (variable), +5V regulated
- Current: 1 amp continous
- Safety: 2A Fuse
